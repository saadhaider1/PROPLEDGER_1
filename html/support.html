Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Profile

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

Can I connect multiple wallets?

Yes, you can connect multiple cryptocurrency wallets to your account for flexibility in transactions.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account, go to Settings > Profile, and update your personal information and preferences.

Is my account information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and blockchain security to protect all user data and transactions.

Investments & Returns

What is the minimum investment amount?

Minimum investment varies by plan: Conservative ($1,000), Balanced ($2,500), Growth ($5,000), Premium ($10,000), Elite ($25,000).

How are returns calculated?

Returns are calculated using compound interest based on your investment plan's APY and are distributed according to your chosen frequency.

When do I receive dividend payments?

Dividend frequency depends on your investment plan: monthly, quarterly, bi-annually, or annually as specified.

Can I withdraw my investment early?

Early withdrawal terms vary by investment plan. Some plans have lock-up periods while others allow flexible withdrawals with potential fees.

Property Purchases

What are fractional shares?

Fractional shares allow you to own a percentage of a property by purchasing tokens representing your ownership stake.

How does rent-to-own work?

Make a down payment and monthly payments over a set term. At the end of the term, you have the option to own the property outright.

Are properties physically inspected?

Yes, all properties undergo professional inspections and due diligence before being listed on our platform.

Can I visit properties before buying?

Virtual tours are available for all properties. Physical visits can be arranged for full ownership purchases.

Blockchain & Tokens

What are PROP tokens?

PROP tokens are ERC-20 tokens that represent your ownership in properties and investments on the PROPLEDGER platform.

How do smart contracts work?

Smart contracts automatically execute transactions and distribute dividends based on predefined rules, ensuring transparency and reliability.

Can I trade PROP tokens?

PROP tokens can be traded on supported exchanges and through our platform's internal marketplace.

What blockchain does PROPLEDGER use?

We currently operate on Ethereum blockchain with plans to expand to other Layer 1 and Layer 2 solutions.

Technical Issues

Why is the website loading slowly?

Clear your browser cache, disable browser extensions, or try accessing from an incognito window. Check your internet connection.

My transaction is stuck pending

Blockchain transactions can take time depending on network congestion. Check the transaction hash on a blockchain explorer.

Is there a mobile app?

Our mobile app is currently in development. For now, our website is fully optimized for mobile browsers.

Browser compatibility issues

We support all modern browsers. For best experience, use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.

Security & Safety

How do I enable 2FA?

Go to Security Settings in your account and follow the setup instructions for two-factor authentication using an authenticator app.

What if I suspect fraudulent activity?

Immediately contact our security team at security@propledger.com and change your password. We'll investigate promptly.

How are funds protected?

Funds are stored in multi-signature wallets with cold storage for maximum security. We also carry insurance coverage.

Can I whitelist withdrawal addresses?

Yes, you can set up whitelisted addresses for withdrawals to enhance security and prevent unauthorized transfers.
